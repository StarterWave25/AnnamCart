AnnamCart Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2025

Welcome to AnnamCart. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you place an order or create an account, we collect:

  • Name
  • Mobile number
  • Email address
  • Delivery address & location
  • Order history and preferences

1.2 Technical & Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Device information (browser, operating system)
  • Pages visited and actions taken
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. Purpose of Collection

We use your information to:

  1. Process and deliver orders
  2. Communicate with you about order status, offers, or support
  3. Personalize your experience (recommendations, saved preferences)
  4. Improve our services through analytics and feedback
  5. Send marketing messages only if you opt in; you can unsubscribe at any time

3. How We Share Your Data

We may share your information with:

  • Restaurants and delivery partners — so they can prepare and deliver your order
  • Payment gateways — to process your online payments securely
  • Third-party service providers (SMS/email platforms, analytics, customer-support tools)
  • Vasuki AI Agent — to help handle your support requests
  • Law enforcement or government authorities, if required by law

We never sell your personal information to unrelated third parties.

4. Data Protection & Security

  • All traffic to and from our site is encrypted via SSL/TLS.
  • We store data on secure servers protected by firewalls.
  • We follow industry-standard security practices (access controls, encryption at rest).
  • Our team undergoes regular security training and audits.

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Correction: You can view or update your personal details in your account settings or by contacting support
  • Data Deletion: You may request that we delete your account and personal data.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe via link in any email or by contacting us.
  • Grievance Officer:
    • Name: Ms. Kavya Rao
    • Email: grievance@annamcart.com
    • We will acknowledge your complaint within 7 days and resolve within 30 days

6. Cookies & Tracking

  • We use cookies to remember your login and preferences, and to improve site performance
  • We use Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to analyze user behavior (anonymous data).
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe we have any such data, please contact us for removal

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised date at the top. Your continued use of AnnamCart after changes indicates your acceptance.
