AnnamCart Delivery & Refund Policy

Last Updated: May 27, 2025

At AnnamCart, we are committed to delivering your food reliably and promptly within Tirumala. Please review our delivery terms and refund process carefully.

1. Delivery Coverage

We currently deliver to all locations within Tirumala.

2. Order Acceptance Window

We accept orders daily between 9:00 AM and 9:00 PM.

Orders are processed and delivered only if the respective restaurant accepts the order.

3. Delivery Time

Estimated delivery time is 40–50 minutes after restaurant confirmation.

However, delivery may be delayed due to:

  • Traffic congestion
  • Weather conditions
  • Delays in restaurant food preparation

4. Delivery Partners

Deliveries are fulfilled by AnnamCart's local delivery partners, who are trained to handle food safely and deliver it efficiently across Tirumala.

5. Customer Responsibilities

Customers must:

  • Be available at the provided delivery address
  • Ensure their phone is reachable
  • Complete the payment (UPI or Cash) directly to the assigned delivery agent

6. Order Not Received / Late Delivery

If you haven’t received your order or it’s significantly delayed:

  1. Open your order tracking page
  2. Click the Help button
  3. Select the relevant issue and submit a report

Our AI agent Vasuki will respond to your concern via WhatsApp

7. Packaging Responsibility

Packaging of food items is managed and provided by the respective restaurant partners. AnnamCart ensures that all packaging meets hygiene and safety standards to the best extent possible.

8. Refund Policy

  • The order is not delivered
  • The food is spoiled
  • Some items are missing
  • Wrong items are delivered (if the cost of delivered items is lower than the order, we refund the difference)
Note:

If the 30-second window has passed and food preparation has started, refunds will not be provided.

All valid refunds are processed within 7 business days.

9. Order Cancellation

You can cancel your order within 30 seconds of placing it. After this time, if the restaurant has already begun preparing the food, the order cannot be canceled or refunded.

If you have any further questions or need help with your order, contact us via the help section on your tracking page or through WhatsApp.
